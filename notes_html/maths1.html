Example Problem:

Solve the following equation: x^2 + 5x + 6 = 0

Solution:

Factorizing the quadratic equation:

(x + 2)(x + 3) = 0

Thus, x = -2 or x = -3

Applications in Engineering:

Mathematics is fundamental to engineering disciplines. Some applications include:

  • Structural analysis in civil engineering
  • Circuit design in electrical engineering
  • Fluid dynamics in mechanical engineering
  • Algorithm development in computer science
