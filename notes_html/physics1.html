Physics I Notes

These are the notes for Physics I in the 2nd Year, SEM 1 of the Basic Engineering course.

Topics Covered:

  • Mechanics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Waves and Oscillations
  • Optics
  • Fluid Mechanics

Key Concepts:

  • Newton's Laws of Motion
  • Thermodynamic Laws
  • Wave Equations and Sound Waves
  • Reflection and Refraction of Light
  • Buoyancy and Archimedes' Principle

Formulas:

  • Force = Mass × Acceleration (F = ma)
  • Work = Force × Distance (W = F × d)
  • Pressure = Force / Area (P = F / A)
  • Gravitational Potential Energy = m × g × h
  • Ohm's Law: V = IR

These notes provide a basic overview of the subject. Further detailed notes can be found in textbooks and other reference materials.
